Privacy Policy

We built OmniPath AI to protect your career data, not exploit it. Here is exactly what we do and don't do with your information.

Last updated: July 2025

Data We Collect

OmniPath AI collects only the information you voluntarily provide during your session to power the AI agents. This includes:

  • Your name and email address from the onboarding form (for personalisation only)
  • Your target role, sector, skills, and experience level to provide contextual career guidance
  • Your resume text, extracted in-memory when you upload a PDF for analysis
  • Your interview responses during a coaching session
  • Your LinkedIn URL and portfolio URL if provided for profile optimisation
All data you provide is used exclusively to power the AI agents on your behalf. It is never used for advertising, profiling, or sold to third parties.
How Data Is Stored

OmniPath AI uses no database. All data lives exclusively in your server-side Flask session, which is:

  • Stored in an encrypted, signed cookie on your browser using a server-side secret key
  • Automatically expired after 1 hour of inactivity — your data is deleted by the server at that point
  • Isolated strictly to your session only — no other user can see your data
  • Never written to disk, a database, or any external service
Because data is session-only, it will be lost when you close your browser, your session expires, or the server restarts. There is no persistence by design.
What We Don't Store

To be explicit about what we will never do:

  • We do not store your resume PDF on our servers — it is parsed in-memory and discarded
  • We do not store your data in any database or file system
  • We do not use analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or third-party scripts that collect data
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party
  • We do not use your data to train AI models
  • We do not require account creation or authentication to use the platform
Data Sharing

OmniPath AI shares zero user data with third parties. The platform runs entirely on self-contained, rule-based heuristic AI logic. There are no calls to external AI APIs (such as OpenAI or Google AI). Your data never leaves the server during processing.

No third parties. No external APIs. No data brokers. Your career data stays completely within this application.
Security Measures

We have implemented enterprise-grade security protections across the entire application stack:

  • HTTP Security Headers (CSP, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options) to prevent XSS and clickjacking
  • Rate Limiting on all API endpoints to prevent abuse and denial-of-service attacks
  • Session Cookie Hardening (HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax) to prevent cookie theft and CSRF attacks
  • PDF Validation — file type, MIME type, magic bytes, and page-count limits to prevent malicious uploads
  • Input Sanitisation — all user inputs are HTML-escaped and length-validated before processing
  • PII Redaction in the infrastructure dashboard — email and URLs are masked before display
  • Secure secret key management via environment variables
Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. Because we use no database, you can delete all of your session data instantly by clearing your session. All profile data, resume analysis results, and execution history will be permanently removed from the server.

Cookies

OmniPath AI uses one single cookie — the Flask session cookie named session. This cookie:

  • Is strictly necessary to maintain your session state during use
  • Contains only an encrypted reference to your server-side session data
  • Is set with HttpOnly and SameSite=Lax security flags
  • Expires after 1 hour of inactivity

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies.

Contact

OmniPath AI is an open-source project built for the Google AI Agents Capstone. If you have any privacy concerns or questions, you can raise an issue in the project repository or contact the project maintainer directly.

This privacy policy applies to the OmniPath AI application as deployed. It may be updated periodically to reflect security improvements.
